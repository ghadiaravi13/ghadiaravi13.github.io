I am a GPU Architect at NVIDIA, Bengaluru, working in the Applied Power Architecture, where we work on analyzing and enhancing GPU performance w.r.t per watt of consumed power, leveraging applied Machine Learning, Deep Learning and Reinforcement Learning. I also work as a part-time research assistant at H2Lab, University of Washington under Prithviraj Ammanabrolu, where I am working on deriving reward from linguistic feedback to train Language Models using RL. My research interests lie in natural language processing and reinforcement learning. Typically, I am interested to work with NLP systems in unsupervised training settings using reinforment learning, in interaction based continual training environments. I am also interested in multi-modal AI, typically at the intersection of Language and Vision.

I graduated from IIT Kharagpur in 2021 with a major in Electronics and Elec. Comm. and a minor in Computer Science. My bachelor thesis was on Training Generative Dialog Models using Reinforcement Learning with Learned Reward Systems, supervised by Dr. Pawan Goyal.

My hobbies include trekking, photography, calisthenics, and a lil bit of cooking. I also love reading scientific blogs related to astronomy and physiology.
